<h1>Dragon Escape</h1>

<h3>Description</h3>

<p>A HTML game where you have to dodge enemies, pick up health and time.  See what your highest score will be!</p>

<h3>Controls</h3>
<p>
    A Key: Move Left</p>
<p>
    D Key: Move Right</p>
<p>
    W Key: Move Up</p>
<p>
    Space Bar: Move Down </p>
